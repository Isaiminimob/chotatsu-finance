Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Chotatsu Finance ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website at chotatsu.org.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you unless you voluntarily provide it to us through our contact form or other communication channels. When you use our website, we may collect non-personal data, such as your IP address, browser type, and usage patterns, to help us understand how our site is used and to improve its functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any non-personal information we collect is used to analyze website traffic, diagnose technical issues, and improve the user experience. If you provide personal information voluntarily, we will only use it to respond to your inquiries or for the purpose for which it was provided. We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

Our website may use "cookies" to enhance your user experience. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device. You can choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you do so, some parts of the site may not function properly.

4. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@chotatsu.org.
